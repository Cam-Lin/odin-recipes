Martini

Description

Are you feeling in a spy-ish mood? This classic drink made famous by the agent 007 has many variations however a classic dirty martini is a must try.

Althrough it can be a jarring experience the first time, don't be discouraged by the olvies in the drink, they will give it the spunk you never knew you needed!

Ingredients:

  • Cocktail glass (chilled)
  • Mixing glass
  • Strainer
  • Olives
  • Olive brine
  • Vodka
  • Dry vermouth

Steps:

  1. Add ice to your mixing glass
  2. Into the mixing glass pour 2 1/2 oz vodka
  3. Add 1/2 oz of dry vermouth
  4. Add 1/2 oz olive brine
  5. Stir in the glass until the drink is chilled
  6. Strain and serve into your cocktail glass
  7. Garnish with an olive
