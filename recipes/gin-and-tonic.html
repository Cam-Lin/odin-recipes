Gin & Tonic

Description

This refreshing drink is a staple for any mixologist, wether at home or at a bar, a gin and tonic is a simple drink that can be made using simple ingredients

Since we're working with very few elements, take into account that their quality will define the taste of the final product.

Ingredients:

  • Tall glass or goblet
  • Ice
  • Lemon wedges
  • Tonic water
  • Gin

Steps:

  1. Take a chilled glass and add some ice
  2. Pour 2 oz of high quality gin
  3. Add 4 oz tonic water
  4. Stir lightly with a bar spoon
  5. Peel the skin off a lemon and twist it, add it to the drink
  6. Garnish with your remaining wedges
