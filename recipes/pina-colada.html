Piña Colada

Description

Cool, refreshing and sweet. What's not to like about this tropical cocktail?

Seemengly a drink made in heaven, enjoy with care, sugary drinks give the worst hangovers.

Ingredients:

  • Poco grande glass
  • Blender
  • Ice
  • Pineapple
  • Pinneapple juice
  • Cherry (for garnish)
  • Coconut cream
  • White rum

Steps:

  1. In the blender mix:
    • A medium piece of pineapple
    • 2 oz coconut cream
    • 2 oz white rum
    • 1 oz pineapple juice
    • Ice
  2. Blend until everything is incorporated
  3. Add more juice to taste until you get the consistency you desire
  4. If you want to add more sweetness, pour some more coconut cream
  5. Pour into the glass and garnish with a piece of pineapple and a cherry on top
